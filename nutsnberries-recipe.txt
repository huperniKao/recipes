Ingredients:
- Shaker
- Rocks glass
- 1 cup of Ice
- 2 oz. Frangelico
- 2 oz. Chambord
- 1 oz. milk (can substitute with whipped cream/ice cream/half n half)

Directions:
1. Fill the rocks glass with ice.
2. Pour in the Frangelico, Chambord, and milk
3. Pour the contents into a shaker and shaker
4. Pour it back into your glass and enjoy.
